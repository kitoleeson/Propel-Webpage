This Client Agreement outlines the policies, expectations, and procedures of Propel Tutoring and applies to all clients -- students and legal guardians (including parents) -- who engage tutoring services through Propel.\\

This Agreement must be signed by the student, and, if the student is less than 18 years of age, by a legal guardian. By signing this Agreement, or by participating in tutoring services, the student and guardian acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined herein.\\

Propel Tutoring reserves the right to update or modify this Agreement. Clients will be notified by email of any material changes; continued participation in tutoring services after such notice constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

\section{Structure of Tutoring Sessions}
Propel Tutoring is a catalyst for students taking ownership of, and investing in, their education. We believe students must have room to explore different learning styles and ways of thinking; because in this exploration, students gain a deeper understanding of their inner processes, setting them up for continued and fulfilling learning. Propel exists to give students the power to choose a style of learning which best suits them, and to break down the principles of math and science in the pursuit of intuitive understanding, not just memorization.\\

Propel Tutoring operates on a student-led model. Students are expected to take an active role in their learning. Students are responsible for:

\begin{itemize}
   \item Determining how session time is used.
   \item Deciding what topics are discussed and what content is reviewed.
   \item Scheduling session dates and times with their tutor.
   \item Communicating changes in scheduling (cancellations or late arrival) with their tutor in a time-efficient manor.
   \item Bringing notes, homework, questions from class, and any other resources or topics they wish to review.
\end{itemize}

Tutors will guide, support, and challenge students, but academic performance and responsibility ultimately rests with the student.

\section{Guardian Involvement}
Research consistently shows that parental involvement plays a critical positive role in students’ academic and personal development. A large meta-analysis synthesizing 448 independent studies found that parents’ naturally occurring involvement in their children’s schooling was positively associated with academic achievement, engagement, and motivation, and that these effects were maintained over time. Parental involvement was also linked to better social adjustment, better emotional adjustment, and lower levels of delinquent behavior. When parents show interest, communicate about school, and remain engaged, students tend to feel more supported, motivated, and emotionally secure. \textit{(The Relation Between Parents’ Involvement in Children’s Schooling and Children’s Adjustment: A Meta-Analysis. Michael M. Barger, Elizabeth Moorman Kim, Nathan R. Kuncel, Eva M. Pomerantz.)}\\

However, research also suggests that excessive or overly controlling parental involvement can undermine student growth. A recent scoping review of 23 peer-reviewed studies on the association between "helicopter parenting" and indices of psychological distress identified over-involved parents to be significantly associated with higher anxiety, depression, and stress among post-secondary students. These negative outcomes are mediated by reduced autonomy, self-efficacy, and self-esteem, suggesting that when parents take too much control, students have fewer opportunities to develop independence, confidence, and responsibility for their own learning. \textit{(The Impact of Helicopter Parenting on Emerging Adults in Higher Education: A Scoping Review of Psychological Adjustment in University Students. Valentina Lucia La Rosa, Boby Ho-Hong Ching, Elena Commodari)}\\

All this to say: current research consistently shows that students benefit most when guardians are involved in ways that are supportive rather than overbearing.\\

In the context of tutoring, guardians are most effective when they contribute to an environment where students are trusted, capable, and empowered to grow. Sometimes this means being an advocate, like when you see the student-tutor relationship is not working. Most often, though, it means offering support and encouragement at a distance and extending grace when students struggle. Tutoring works best when students and tutors communicate openly, trust one another, and have a positive working relationship.\\

For students under 16 years of age, it is recommended that a guardian is present for the first session with a new tutor. After the first session, so long as the student is comfortable, it is recommended that students be given space to work alone with their tutors so that we can cultivate an environment focused on deepening understanding and intuitive learning removed from other expectations.

\section{Pricing Overview}
Each tutor at Propel has their own hourly rate, determined by experience, education, and tutoring background. Rates may vary between tutors. All tutor rates are presented when a student is choosing their tutor, and will be confirmed with you by email once chosen. Once a student begins working with a tutor, that particular tutor’s hourly rate for that specific student will never increase, even if it increases for new clients in the future.

\subsection{Invoicing}
Invoices will be sent out biweekly, directly to the email address of the primary billing account specified in the intake form (could be a guardian or a student $\ge$ 18 years old).\\

All sessions are billed in increments of 15 minutes, rounded up or down to the nearest 0.25 hours. Tutors track session length and date internally, and each invoice will summarize both metrics for it's two-week span.\\

All payments are due within 7 days of receiving the invoice. This is to ensure our tutors are paid on time for their work. Payments can be made via Interac e-Transfer to the email provided at the top of each invoice. Failure to submit timely payment may result in a pause or termination of tutoring services, and a late payment penalty of 10\% of the original amount each week until the payment is made in full.

\subsection{First Session Policy}
The first session between a student and a new tutor is free of charge. This allows both parties to confirm that the tutoring relationship is a good fit before moving forward. We encourage students to make an informed decision when choosing their tutor, but this policy provides extra information to confirm that decision.\\

In the event that the tutoring relationship is not a good fit, students may contact Propel directly to change tutors at \href{mailto:propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}{propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}. If a student does not believe they have found a good tutoring relationship with the first two tutors for any subject, they will need to pay full price for the first session with a third tutor and beyond. This policy is meant to ensure the relationship is a good fit, not to give free tutoring to students who will switch tutors after every session.

\subsection{Small Group Sessions}
Multiple students may wish to book one or more sessions together with a tutor. Small group sessions will be considered on a case-by-case basis.\\

The most value a student will get from their time will always be a one-on-one session with a tutor, however in some cases small group sessions may be a good option. In the case of a group session, each additional student will add 0.5 of the tutor's rate on top of the base rate. For example, a group session with two students will be billed at 1.5x the tutor's rate, and a group session with three students will be billed at 2x the tutor's rate.\\

Small group sessions must be scheduled directly through the company, not the individual tutor. Queries regarding group sessions can be sent to \href{mailto:propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}{propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}.

\subsection{Where Does the Money Go?}
Propel Tutoring exists first and foremost to help students succeed, not maximize profit. We recognize that education is a major investment of time, energy, and financial resources, and we do not take that lightly. Our pricing model is designed to be fair, transparent, and grounded in the belief that high-quality tutoring requires tutors to be compensated appropriately for their time, expertise, and preparation.\\

For each tutoring session, a flat \$5 per hour is retained by Propel Tutoring and used to support business operations including infrastructure, systems, tutor support, and ongoing improvements to the tutoring experience. The remainder is paid directly to the tutor for their work.

\section{Scheduling}
All sessions times and locations will be scheduled together by the tutor and student (and possibly guardian). We recommend setting up a weekly recurring tutoring session. From that basis, sessions can be added or removed based on the student's needs. This form of scheduling makes tutoring a consistent part of the student's week, promoting accountability and making planning easier.

\subsection{Rescheduling \& Cancellations}
We understand that life can be busy and that unexpected conflicts arise from time to time. At the same time, tutoring sessions require preparation and time commitments from both students and tutors, and it is important that each party’s time is respected. For this reason, Propel Tutoring has clear and consistent policies regarding rescheduling and cancellations.\\

For each session, a "pivot time" is set at 22:00 MT the evening before the session if the scheduled start time is at or prior to 10:00 MT the following day, or two hours before the scheduled start time if the scheduled start time is later than 10:00 MT the following day.\\

If intention to reschedule or cancel is communicated prior to this pivot time, regardless of whether it comes from the student or tutor, no penalty will apply. However, if a tutor presents intention to reschedule or cancel a session after this pivot time, the client’s next session will be billed at half price; and if a student presents intention to reschedule or cancel after this pivot time, the client will be charged for an extra half hour for the next session.\\

The most important thing here is communication. Both student and tutor should be in contact with the other if they will be late, need to reschedule, or need to cancel, preferably as early before the session as possible. We hope that these measures will seldom need to be used.\\

If either party is more than 10 minutes late without communication, the session is treated as a cancellation. In the event of lateness with timely communication, the best case scenario is to have a full length session, starting as soon as both people are present. If either party is unable to make this work, then the session will be billed only for as long as the session goes for (rounded to the nearest 0.25 hours, as per usual).\\

Tutors are responsible for reporting all rescheduling and cancellations to Propel, regardless of whether it was initiated by the tutor or student.

\section{Choosing and Changing a Tutor}
Propel Tutoring places a strong emphasis on allowing students to choose their own tutor. We believe that learning is most effective when students feel comfortable, respected, and genuinely connected to the person supporting them. This model allows the student to find a tutor who will work best for themselves. Some points of consideration may be: shared interests, academic goals, intended fields of study, previous schools attended, practical factors such as location and availability, et cetera. This process empowers students to take an active role in their education and increases the likelihood that they will be engaged, motivated, and invested in their sessions.\\

Students select their tutor prior to signing this Agreement. If, at any point, a student wishes to change tutors, they may contact Propel Tutoring by email at \href{mailto:propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}{propeltutoringyeg@gmail.com}. Please include the name of your current tutor and the name of the tutor you wish to switch to in your email. Information and bios for all tutors (both those currently accepting clients and not) are available on our website at all times. Tutor changes will be discussed by request to ensure a smooth and appropriate transition.

\subsection{Conflict Management}
Disagreements happen, but if any major conflicts arise between student and tutor, please do not hesitate to reach out directly to Propel via email. We are dedicated to ensuring your tutoring relationship is productive and conducive to your educational success, and are happy to assist you in navigating any conflicts.
